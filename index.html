Garage

Fuel · Maintenance · Insurance · Inspection — on your iPhone.

Garage is the simple way to track everything your car costs you. Snap a fuel-pump receipt and Garage reads the gallons, price, and date for you. Your MPG shows up on the dashboard automatically. When the oil change is coming due, when the registration sticker expires, when your insurance renews — Garage knows.

Privacy-first by design

  • Your data lives on your iPhone. iCloud sync is opt-in and end-to-end encrypted by Apple.
  • No analytics. No tracking. No third-party SDKs. We can't see what you log because we don't run a server.
  • VIN numbers and insurance policy numbers are stored in Apple's Keychain.

Pricing

  • Free — 1 vehicle, basic reminders, last 30 fuel logs.
  • Pro — $3.99/mo or $24.99/yr (7-day trial). Multi-car, iCloud, full reminders, CSV mileage export.
  • Pro+ — $7.99/mo or $49.99/yr (14-day trial). Unlimited cars, 12-month cost forecast, outlier alerts, Family Sharing.
