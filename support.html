Frequently asked

Why doesn't my MPG show up?

MPG needs two consecutive full-tank fuel-ups, with the second odometer higher than the first. Half-tank fill-ups are intentionally excluded so they don't muddy your average. Toggle "Full tank" on when you fill all the way.

I drive electric — does this work?

Yes. Set fuel type to "Electric" on your vehicle. Garage tracks kWh and mi/kWh instead of gallons and MPG. The dashboard math adapts automatically.

How do I restore my subscription on a new phone?

Open Garage → Settings → Restore Purchases. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, your tier comes back instantly.

Where are my receipts stored? Can someone else read them?

Receipt photos live in the SwiftData store on your iPhone, protected by Apple's file-protection (the data is encrypted while the phone is locked). If you turn on iCloud sync, photos sync to your private iCloud database via Apple's CloudKit. We can't read them.

Can I share Garage with my spouse / household?

Yes — turn on Apple Family Sharing for Pro+. Family members get the same Pro+ entitlement at no extra cost; each person has their own private vehicle list synced via their own iCloud.

Why does inspection say my state doesn't require a test?

Garage ships the most current dataset we could verify at submission. Rules vary by county and change over time — check your local DMV for the authoritative answer. We'll refresh the dataset in App Store updates.

How do I delete a vehicle?

Swipe left on the row in the Vehicles tab → Archive. Archived vehicles keep their history but drop out of the dashboard and statistics. From the archive view you can permanently delete or restore.

The receipt scanner missed a field.

Tap the field and type the correct value — Garage trusts your edits. Some receipts (faded thermal paper, glare, weird angles) are tough; take a steadier shot in better light.
